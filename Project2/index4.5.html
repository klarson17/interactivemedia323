Fumbling, stumbling,


Fighting, igniting,


Blinded, reminded,


Turning, yearning,


Soaking, choking,


Hiding, abiding,


Chasing, racing,


Breathless, restless,


Dreaming, gleaming,


Winning, new beginning,


around in the dark.


flames from a spark.


of the fear inside.


for a place to hide.


struggling to breathe.


my ghost won't leave.


I stagger away.


in the mud I lay.


I escape the past.


I am free at last.
